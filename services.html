Precision Agriculture & Land Title Clarity for Telangana

The prosperity of **Telangana's agriculture** relies on verifiable land records. We offer end-to-end surveying support tailored to the state's unique farming practices and legal framework. Our commitment: **We will come and survey your agriculture land throughout every village and district in Telangana.**

Key Agricultural Services:

  • **Precision Boundary Demarcation:** Resolving plot line disputes with sub-centimeter accuracy for clear ownership and transfer.
  • **Topographic & Contour Mapping:** Essential for optimizing irrigation, drainage systems, and high-efficiency farm planning.
  • **Area Certification for Compliance:** Providing certified land extent for crop insurance, bank loans, and government subsidy schemes.

Civil Engineering & High-Tolerance Infrastructure

Our DGPS control network provides the foundational geospatial data necessary for large-scale, critical infrastructure projects. We ensure your construction aligns perfectly with design specifications, mitigating costly errors.

Infrastructure Applications:

  • **Route Surveys:** Accurate alignment and profile mapping for roads, railway lines, pipelines, and transmission corridors.
  • **Volume & Earthwork Calculations:** Precise 'Cut and Fill' volumetric analysis for site grading, excavation, and stockyard management.
  • **Construction Layout/Staking:** Using DGPS to precisely transfer design plans onto the ground, guiding construction activities.

Regulatory Compliance & Certified Deliverables

Our commitment extends beyond field accuracy; we ensure full legal and digital integration with regional authorities.

Telangana Land Records (Dharani Portal)

We prepare all necessary supporting documentation, including **ROR-1B and Pahani sketches**, formatted to seamlessly integrate with the **Dharani Portal** and MRO/Tahsildar offices.

Certified Digital Outputs

All surveys result in legally certified reports and clean digital files: **Digital Maps (.DWG/.DXF), Google Earth KMLs, and Geo-referenced PDF plots**, ready for immediate use by architects and engineers.

Data Integrity Audit

Every dataset undergoes a rigorous internal quality assurance (QA) audit, verifying **spatial consistency** and adherence to national and state geodetic standards before certification.
