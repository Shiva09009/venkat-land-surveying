The Apex of Accuracy: DGPS & RTK Geomatic Solutions

Conventional GPS accuracy is insufficient for legal or engineering requirements. We leverage Differential Global Positioning System (DGPS) and Real-Time Kinematic (RTK) corrections to overcome standard error sources (atmospheric refraction, satellite clock drift). This advanced methodology guarantees $<2$ cm positional accuracy, a metric critical for precise legal boundary demarcation, high-tolerance construction layout, and complex infrastructure design. We deploy professional-grade GNSS Receivers and integrate data with established Ground Control Points (GCPs) for verifiable and stable geospatial data integrity.
